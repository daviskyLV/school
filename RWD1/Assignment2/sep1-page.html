Semester Project 1 (SEP1)

All courses on the first 4 semesters are compulsory, and they all include a semester project. The overall goal of the semester projects is to connect the courses and for students to apply the skills acquired during the semester. Project methods, teamwork, communication, and documentation skills are taught in the context of the semester projects.


Each semester has a theme. The themes of the first four semesters are:

  • 1st semester: Single User Systems
  • 2nd semester: Client/Server Systems
  • 3rd semester: Heterogeneous Systems
  • 4th semester: Internet of Things
